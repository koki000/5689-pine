About

Pinegonia is a one-man indie game studio established in 2017 with the initial idea of making simple mobile games for Android and iOS. Led by creativity and passion for puzzle games, Pinegonia has released its first game on Google Play: Memory in Orbit. This charming memory game is great for boosting your memory power, training your attention and strengthening your cognitive skills.
